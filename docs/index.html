An artisan soap brand, lovingly crafting soap from vintage recipes in the historic city of Wells

Horrington Handmade Soaps

Homegrown ingredients. Exotic scents. Skill, passion, and a love of cleanliness. ❤ Love.

These are what go into our handmade soaps. We started from our own kitchen, in a return-to-nature moment inspired by long hikes through the English countryside outside our back door.

Where to buy?

You can find our beautiful soaps in the Wells Cathedral Gift Shop, as well as contact us directly to order or occasionally at the Wednesday and Saturday Wells market. We’re a young business, currently setting up our online storefront.

We believe we can clean our hands, dishes, hearts and socks while also keeping the world clean. Bubbling with joy,

– Tara & Oli

We also have a more detailed ingredients list, all natural and hypoallergenic! If you’d like to try soapmaking yourself, we sometimes run workshops.
